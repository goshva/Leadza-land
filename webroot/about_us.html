Our Story
5 years ago we started as an advertising agency helping small and medium businesses run their first advertising campaigns on Facebook&Instagram. Our team optimised millions of dollars of ad spent and tried lot's of optimization techniques while improving our technical background. Since 2016 we started to build software platforms to help businesses advertise smarter.

Today Leadza is a self-service platform that provides advertisers with customizable optimizations and hires virtual assistants to manage and optimize Facebook&Instagram campaigns. On average our users (startups, merchants and marketers) save up to 50% of the manual work and improve their performance up to 40%.

We are a seed-stage company and happy to have IIDF (the most active early-stage venture fund in Europe with $100M under management and 350+ deals ranging from $20k to $5.5M) and Aitarget (AI-powered SwaS (Software WITH a Service) that helps advertisers automate routine operations, scale FB&IG campaigns) as investors that help us create the best assistance platform for Facebook advertisers.
